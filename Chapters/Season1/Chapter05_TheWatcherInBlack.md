Chapter 05 — The Watcher In Black 

Summary

After surviving the first stabilized distortion zone, Kairo returns to the Tenshirō estate carrying disturbing revelations about the entities emerging from beyond reality.

As Tokyo descends further into fear and instability, the clan realizes the distortions are not random phenomena—but deliberate fractures connected to Ama-no-Mikaboshi’s awakening.

Meanwhile, Reika struggles with growing terror toward her own Seraphic Eye after its public manifestation beneath the blood moon.

As tensions rise within the clan, Kaze finally reveals part of the hidden truth behind the Celestial Eyes, the Silent Heaven, and the ancient pact that created the bloodlines themselves.

And for the first time—

the words “Trinity Resonance” are spoken openly.
