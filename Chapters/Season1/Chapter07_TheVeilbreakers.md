Chapter 07 — The Veilbreakers

Summary

In the aftermath of the celestial fracture, public panic spreads across Japan as governments, clans, and hidden organizations scramble to understand what has happened.

While Reika remains unconscious after her connection to the fracture intensifies, Kairo becomes increasingly determined to uncover the truth behind the Veilbreakers.

As investigations continue, the Tenshirō Clan learns disturbing information about the group's origins, goals, and connection to the ancient history of Ama-no-Mikaboshi.

Meanwhile, Zenith begins openly moving pieces across the board, revealing that the recent events were never random.

They were planned.

Opening Scene

The world changed in a single night.

By morning, the celestial fracture dominated every news broadcast on Earth.

Governments denied it.

Scientists argued over it.

Religious groups preached about it.

And ordinary people simply stared upward in fear.

The silver crack remained visible even during daylight hours.

Faint.

Thin.

Impossible to ignore.

Tokyo had become the center of global attention.

Inside the Tenshirō estate, emergency protocols remained active around the clock.

Additional barriers surrounded the compound.

Patrols doubled.

No one trusted the silence anymore.

Especially not Kairo.

He stood outside the medical wing, staring through a reinforced observation window.

Reika remained unconscious.

Three days had passed since the fracture event.

Three days.

And she still had not awakened.

The golden cracks beneath her skin occasionally pulsed with faint light before fading again.

Every time they did, the monitors surrounding her reacted strangely.

As though her body no longer fully obeyed normal rules.

Kairo clenched his fists.

He hated feeling helpless.

The door behind him opened quietly.

Kaze entered.

Neither spoke immediately.

Both already knew what the other was thinking.

Finally, Kairo broke the silence.

“Tell me about the Veilbreakers.”

Kaze's expression hardened instantly.

Not with anger.

With memory.

And that worried Kairo far more.

Because it meant the answer was older than he expected.
