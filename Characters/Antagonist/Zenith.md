---

## Overview

Zenith is a former elder of the Tenshirō Clan and the central philosophical antagonist behind the Veilbreakers movement.

Once admired for his wisdom and talent, Zenith uncovered the forbidden truth behind the Tenshirō bloodline and the existence of Ama-no-Mikaboshi.

Believing the clan had become jailers of divinity rather than inheritors of freedom, he rebelled against the celestial order itself.

Twenty years ago, his Abyss Eye lost control during the Sealing Incident, forcing Kaze to seal him away.

Now returned, Zenith seeks to liberate both humanity and Mikaboshi from fate itself.
