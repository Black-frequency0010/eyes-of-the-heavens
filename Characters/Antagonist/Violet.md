## Title
The Unchosen | Fate Breaker

---

## Basic Information

- Clan: Former Tenshirō Branch Family (Exiled)
- Age: 21
- Height: 175 cm (5'9")
- Role: Rogue Heir / Veilbreaker Operative
- Nature: Chaos Sovereignty / Defiance
- Eye: Fractured Eye (Rengoku no Me) — Right Eye Only
- Status: The Unchosen

---

## Overview

Violet is an exiled descendant of the Tenshirō bloodline whose ocular power manifested incorrectly.

Unlike Kairo’s Heavenly Eye, which aligns and rewrites fate, Violet’s Fractured Eye destroys destiny structures entirely.

He became one of the Veilbreakers’ strongest operatives after discovering the truth behind the clan’s hidden history.

Though openly antagonistic toward Kairo, Violet’s true hatred is directed toward fate itself.

## Fractured Eye — Rengoku no Me

A cracked amethyst eye filled with shifting fractures and unstable probability lines.

The Fractured Eye governs:
- chaos
- defiance
- broken destiny
- probability collapse

---

## Abilities

### Probability Fracture
Breaks favorable outcomes into catastrophic ones.

### Fate Defiance
Temporarily disrupts nearby ocular powers.

### Chaos Bloom
Creates chain reactions from minor events.

### Destiny Collapse
Destabilizes future prediction systems.

### Zero Alignment
Forces fate-based powers into temporary dormancy.
