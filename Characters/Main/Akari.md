## Overview

Akari is the youngest member of the primary Tenshirō bloodline and the wielder of the Oracle Eye.

Unlike Kairo and Reika, whose powers dominate reality directly, Akari perceives the invisible “threads” connecting people, emotions, and future events.

She serves as the clan’s strategist, warning system, and emotional observer.

Though gentle by nature, Akari constantly fears becoming irrelevant beside her overwhelmingly powerful siblings.

---
