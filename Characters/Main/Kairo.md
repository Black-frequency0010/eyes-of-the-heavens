The Chosen One | Heavenly Eye Heir

- Clan: Tenshirō Clan
- Age: 19
- Height: 189 cm (6'2")
- Role: Heir Apparent / Supreme Strategist

- Eye: Heavenly Eye (Tenkūgan) — Left Eye Only
- Status: The Chosen One

---

## Overview

Kairo is the firstborn son and believed heir of the Tenshirō Clan. Born with the Heavenly Eye in his left eye, he is viewed as a miracle blessed by the heavens themselves.

The Heavenly Eye activates only during moments of extreme danger, rewriting reality itself to fulfill the user’s deepest desire: victory.

Calm, intelligent, and terrifyingly composed, Kairo believes destiny itself bends in his favor.

---

## Personality

- Calm
- Strategic
- Emotionally restrained
- Extremely confident
- Deeply protective of Reika
- Quietly afraid of losing his humanity

Kairo rarely panics. Even under pressure, he maintains control and trusts the Heavenly Eye completely.

---

## Heavenly Eye — Tenkūgan

A divine ocular power unique to Kairo.

The eye observes:
- Past
- Present
- Future

When these three points align vertically, the Heavenly Eye awakens fully.
