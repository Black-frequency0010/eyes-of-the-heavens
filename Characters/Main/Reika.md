---

- Clan: Tenshirō Clan

- Height: 172 cm (5'8")
- Role: Second Heir / Divine Executor

- Eye: Seraphic Eye (Seirai no Me) — Left Eye Only

---

## Overview

Reika is Kairo’s younger twin sister and the wielder of the Seraphic Eye — a holy ocular power so overwhelming that many within the clan fear her more than any enemy.

Her existence represents divine judgment itself.

Where Kairo rewrites reality and Kaze anchors it, Reika purifies it.

Her power grows stronger in the presence of corruption, evil, or chaos.

---

## Personality

- Elegant
- Calm
- Ruthlessly compassionate
- Emotionally dependent on Kairo
- Terrified of her own power
- Secretly enjoys divine judgment

Despite her serenity, Reika constantly fears becoming the “Divine Calamity.”

---

## Seraphic Eye — Seirai no Me

A radiant golden-white eye with:
- six angelic wings orbiting the iris
- a cross-shaped pupil
- overwhelming holy aura

The eye judges:
- sin
- corruption
- impurity
- malice
