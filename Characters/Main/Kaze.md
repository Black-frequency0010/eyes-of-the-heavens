- Clan: Tenshirō Clan
- Age: 47
- Height: 193 cm (6'4")
- Role: Clan Head / Supreme Sovereign
- Nature: Galactic Dominion / Stellar Sovereignty
- Eye: Celestial Eye (Seiun no Me) — Both Eyes

---

## Overview

Kaze is the patriarch of the Tenshirō Clan and the previous generation’s miracle.

Where Kairo rewrites fate and Reika purifies it, Kaze anchors it.

His Celestial Eye draws power from the stars, galaxies, and the cosmic structure of fate itself. He serves as the stabilizing force preventing the twins’ overwhelming powers from collapsing reality.

Many within the clan believe Kaze carries the literal weight of heaven on his shoulders.

---

## Personality

- Stoic

- Emotionally burdened
- Extremely disciplined
- Protective but distant
- Obsessed with maintaining balance

Kaze loves his children deeply, but often treats them as responsibilities before family.

## Celestial Eye — Seiun no Me

A cosmic ocular power connected to:
- stars
- galaxies
- constellations
- fate structures

The eyes appear as swirling nebulae filled with rotating star systems.

The Celestial Eye perceives the “grand design” of reality itself.
