## Overview

Kaze is the patriarch of the Tenshirō Clan and the previous generation’s miracle.

Where Kairo rewrites fate and Reika purifies it, Kaze anchors it.

His Celestial Eye draws power from the stars, galaxies, and the cosmic structure of fate itself. He serves as the stabilizing force preventing the twins’ overwhelming powers from collapsing reality.

Many within the clan believe Kaze carries the literal weight of heaven on his shoulders.
