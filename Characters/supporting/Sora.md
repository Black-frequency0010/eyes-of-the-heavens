# Sora

## Title
The Memory Archivist | Keeper of Echoes

---

## Basic Information

- Clan: Tenshirō Clan
- Age: 24
- Height: 178 cm (5'10")
- Role: Archivist / Psychological Support
- Nature: Memory Sovereignty / Historical Echoes
- Eye: Memoria Eye (Kiokugan) — Both Eyes
- Status: The One Who Remembers

---

## Overview

Sora is the clan’s memory archivist and one of the few individuals who understands fragments of the true history behind the Tenshirō bloodline.

During the Sealing Incident 20 years ago, he accidentally absorbed fractured memories connected to Ama-no-Mikaboshi and Zenith’s fall.

Since then, Sora has carried unbearable psychological burdens hidden beneath a calm exterior.

He serves as the keeper of forbidden truths.

---

## Personality

- Quiet
- Intelligent
- Emotionally exhausted
- Deeply empathetic
- Secretly resentful
- Traumatized by knowledge

Sora often isolates himself within the archives to avoid overwhelming emotional noise from absorbed memories.

---

## Memoria Eye — Kiokugan

A silver-gray ocular power capable of:
- viewing memories
- preserving history
- extracting emotional echoes
- reconstructing forgotten events

The eye perceives time through memory rather than fate.

---
