# Haruto

The Blade Warden | Unyielding Guardian

---

- Clan: Tenshirō Clan (Sworn Guardian Branch)
- Age: 28
- Height: 185 cm (6'1")
- Role: Protector / Elite Enforcer
- Nature: Martial Sovereignty / Severance
- Eye: Blade Eye (Zankigan) — Right Eye Only
- Status: The Unyielding Blade

---

## Overview

Haruto is the loyal guardian of the Tenshirō heirs and one of the clan’s strongest frontline combatants.

Unlike the overwhelmingly cosmic abilities of Kairo, Reika, and Kaze, Haruto’s power specializes in severance — the ability to cut through powers, concepts, and supernatural structures themselves.

He acts as the grounded protector of the family, often serving as the stabilizing force during internal conflicts.

---

- nullification

## Abilities

### Ability Severance
Can temporarily cut or disable ocular powers.

### Absolute Defense
Creates invisible cutting barriers around targets.

### Precision Strike
Cuts weaknesses within techniques or structures.

### Void Edge
A single slash capable of severing cause-and-effect momentarily.
