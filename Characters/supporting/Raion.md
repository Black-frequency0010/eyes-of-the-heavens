# Raion

## Title
The Beast Warden | Untamed Guardian

---

## Basic Information

- Clan: Tenshirō Protector Branch
- Age: 26
- Height: 190 cm (6'3")
- Role: Beast Tamer / Frontline Vanguard
- Nature: Primal Sovereignty / Instinctual Dominion
- Eye: Feral Eye (Yasei no Me) — Both Eyes
- Status: The Untamed Guardian

---

## Overview

Raion is one of the Tenshirō Clan’s strongest physical combatants and a childhood companion of the heirs.

His Feral Eye grants overwhelming instinctual combat ability and dominion over primal beast forces. While others wield cosmic or conceptual powers, Raion embodies raw survival and natural aggression.

He often acts as the frontline shield protecting the more fragile strategists and ocular users.

---

## Personality

- Loyal
- Straightforward
- Protective
- Emotionally honest
- Easily frustrated by politics
- Quietly insecure about his intelligence

Raion sometimes feels inferior beside the god-like powers of the main heirs.

---

## Feral Eye — Yasei no Me

Golden animalistic eyes with slit pupils and glowing instinct patterns.

The eye enhances:
- reflexes
- senses
- regeneration
- primal dominance

## Abilities

### Beast Dominion
Controls or intimidates supernatural beasts.

### Instinct Acceleration
Moves and reacts faster than conscious thought.

### Primal Regeneration
Rapidly heals physical damage during combat.

### Savage Manifestation
Temporarily transforms into a monstrous combat state.

### Predator’s Territory
Creates an area where enemy instincts collapse under fear pressure.
