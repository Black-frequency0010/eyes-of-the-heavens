## Overview

Luna Voss is a mysterious outsider who appeared shortly after a catastrophic clan incident.

Neither ally nor enemy, Luna exists between opposing forces. Her Eclipse Eye embodies perfect duality — light and darkness, creation and destruction, order and chaos.

Her presence destabilizes the balance of every major faction around her.

She is especially drawn toward Kairo and Reika due to their overwhelming resonance with celestial power.

## Eclipse Eye — Shokugan

A dual-colored ocular power:
- Left Eye: pitch-black with silver stars
- Right Eye: blazing white with golden rings

When synchronized, both eyes create an eclipse phenomenon.

The Eclipse Eye governs:
- balance
- inversion
- neutralization
- duality
