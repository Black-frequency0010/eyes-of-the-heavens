# Dr. Yumi Kagami

## Title
The Heretic Scientist | Architect of Forbidden Evolution

---

## Basic Information

- Clan: None
- Age: 32
- Height: 170 cm (5'7")
- Role: Scientist / Ocular Research Specialist
- Nature: Artificial Evolution / Forbidden Knowledge
- Eye: None
- Status: The Heretic Researcher

---

## Overview

Dr. Yumi Kagami is one of the world’s leading researchers on ocular phenomena and supernatural evolution.

Brilliant but morally ambiguous, Yumi seeks to understand the true origin of the Eyes — even if it means violating ethical boundaries.

Unlike most researchers, she does not worship the Tenshirō bloodline. She views them as biological systems carrying dangerous conceptual entities.

Her obsession with the Seraphic Eye and Trinity resonance has placed her under constant surveillance by multiple factions.

---

## Personality

- Highly intelligent
- Curious to a dangerous degree
- Emotionally detached
- Calm under pressure
- Morally flexible
- Secretly fascinated by divinity

Yumi prioritizes knowledge over comfort, law, or tradition.

---

## Research Specialization

Yumi studies:
- ocular evolution
- resonance phenomena
- corruption mechanics
- celestial fragments
- forbidden synchronization experiments

She is one of the few people attempting to artificially reproduce ocular powers.
